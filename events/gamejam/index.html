SGDC Game Jam IX

Come down to Lieb 120 for the Opening and Closing ceremonies of our Jam!

Opening Ceremony: September 23rd at 7pm

  • Learn the theme of the jam
  • Eat food
  • Form teams
  • Develop together

Closing Ceremony: September 25th at 7pm

  • Games are due before this
  • Present and play submissions
  • Eat food
  • Vote on winners
  • Win prizes
  • Giveaways

Jam Rules and Procedures

  • Games have to be made during the game jam
  • Games can be of all kinds -- board games, social games, video games, etc. Game mods are admissible.
  • Asset steals must be credited in a text document submitted with your game. Steals from yourself count as asset steals
  • Upload your game to itch.io before the deadline
  • Placements and Prizes
  • 1st place - $50
  • 2nd place - $40
  • 3rd place - $25
  • Best Visual Design - $20
  • Best Audio Design - $20
  • Best Use of Theme - $20
